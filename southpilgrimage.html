South Pilgrimage Tour Package

Discover the spiritual allure of South India on our Pilgrimage Tour Package, as you visit ancient temples, seek blessings, and delve into the rich religious traditions that define the region's profound cultural heritage.

Learn more
Day 1 : Madurai
After your arrival at Madurai Railway station or Airport, you will be picked by our tour representatives and transferred to the hotel. Get into the hotel and relax for sometime. In the evening, get ready for a sightseeing tour to Madurai. View the famous Meenakshi Sanctuary, Thirupparankundram Sanctuary, Thirumalai Nayakar Royal residence, Alagar Koil and Gandhi Display Center. Spend your overnight stay at the hotel in Madurai.
Day 2 : Madurai - Rameshwaram (175 KMS - 04 Hours drive)
Take your breakfast and move ahead to Rameshwaram. After the reach, get into the hotel and enjoy at your leisure. Move ahead for touring to Rameshwaram and visit Ramanathaswamy Sanctuary, Ramjharoka Temple, Agnitheertham, dhanushkodi and Adam's Scaffold. Return back to the hotel in Rameshwaram for overnight stay.
Day 3 : Rameshwaram - Kanyakumari (250 KMS - 06 Hours drive)
After morning breakfast check out from the lodging and drive to Kanyakumari. On reaching, check into the inn. In the noon, get ready for a tour to Kanyakumari and visit Devi Kanyakumari Sanctuary, Vivekananda Rock, Suchithram Sanctuary, Gandhi Dedication and Triveni Sangam. Spend your overnight stay by watching the lovely nightfall of Kanyakumari.
Day 4 : Kanyakumari - Trivandrum
After morning breakfast, check out from the hotel and drive to Trivandrum. On the way, visit the Sri Padmanabhapuram Castle and explore great knowledge. Check out the Sri Padmanabhaswamy Sanctuary in Trivandrum which is the wealthiest sanctuary on the planet, Historical Center, Secretariat and so on.
Day 5 : Departure
Later, our tour representatives will drop you to the Air terminal/Track Station for forward trip.
