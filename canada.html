Canadian Rockies

Experience the awe-inspiring beauty of the Canadian Rockies on our immersive tour package, featuring stunning landscapes, turquoise lakes, and exhilarating outdoor adventures.

Day 1 : Arrival Toronto -City tour
After your arrival at Toronto Airport, our tour representatives will meet and transfer you to the hotel. Get ready for a city tour to Toronto. You will be visiting the home of the Stanley Cup and the Hockey Hall of Fame. Ensure to check out the Rogers Centre which is considered to be the dynamic and versatile entertainment centre in the world. Proceed to famous CN Tower where you need to travel in the glass elevator to the 1,122 foot glass floored outdoor observation deck. Just enjoy a great time in this amazing city.
Day 2 : Ottawa City tour
After a quick breakfast, get ready to visit the Kingston. Upon your arrival, you will be enjoying a cruise of the 1000 islands from Kingston that offers a great insight of the destination. Later, move to Ottawa and visit the Parliament Hill which is regarded as the seat of Canadaâ€™s national government. Visit The Rideau Canal, Ontario's first UNESCO World Heritage Site and much more.
Day 3 : Montreal - the Paris of North America.
Take your breakfast and get into a luxurious coach to drive to Montreal. Visit the beautiful Notre Dame which is known for its paintings, statues, beautiful interior, gold-tipped polychrome carvings, rich altarpiece and much more. You can take some photo shots on top of Mont Royal and overlook Montreal from the top.
Day 4 : Quebec
After your breakfast, drive to Quebec for a great city tour. Our tour guide will narrate you the history and tour you to the walled city in North America. Ensure to check out the Parliament Buildings, Upper and Lower Towns, the Plains of Abraham, Chateau Frontenac, Place d'Armes, the Citadel and Quebec National Assembly.
Day 5 : Quebec-Calgary
Take your American breakfast in the morning. Board a flight to Calgary and get the assistance of our tour representatives. You would be visiting one of the most beautiful cities of Canada. Just look over the magnificent Heritage Park, the Olympic Park and much more. Get transferred to beautiful city of Banff with its charming streets and spectacular natural beauty.
Day 6 : Icefield Parkway
After a quick breakfast, get into the hotel and relax for sometime. The journey will start towards the Icefield Parkway and you would be moving around the precipitous gorges, snowcapped summits and lakes of exquisite blues. Just stop at the ice field and stand on the ice. You would be able to look the glaciers face to face and also experience the Glacier adventure. Later, you will be stopped at Johnston Canyon to enjoy the real fun.
