Day 1 : Cochin - Munnar (160 Kms. 03 ½ Hrs.)
After your arrival at Cochin Airport, our tour agent will greet and transfer you to Munnar, the gateway of hills in South India. On the way, visit the Cheeyappara Waterfalls and watch the lovely and bright blooms in blossom Garden. Later, go back to hotel, take rest for some time and investigate the magnificence of the nature. Move back to the hotel in Munnar for overnight stay.
Day 2 : Munnar
After breakfast, get ready for a morning touring trek to Munnar which incorporates, Mattupetty Dam, Eravikulam National Park, Echo Point, Kundala Dam, View Point and the Tea Gardens. Get back to the hotel for overnight stay in Munnar.
Day 3 : Munnar - Thekkady (110 Kms - 03 Hrs)
Take your breakfast and check-out from the inn. Move ahead to Thekkady. Get into the hotel and relax for sometime. After a short break, proceed for Thekkady tour which incorporates, Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary, Hill Station and Spice Plantation. Trekking, Bamboo Rafting and Elephant Ride. Spend your overnight stay at the hotel.
Day 4 : Thekkady - Cochin (190 Kms. - 04 Hrs.)
After your morning breakfast, check-out from the inn and exchange to Cochin Airport/Railway station. Get into the flight/train with a ton of sweet memories to reach your destination.
Day 6 : Icefield Parkway
After a quick breakfast, get into the hotel and relax for sometime. The journey will start towards the Icefield Parkway and you would be moving around the precipitous gorges, snowcapped summits and lakes of exquisite blues. Just stop at the ice field and stand on the ice. You would be able to look the glaciers face to face and also experience the Glacier adventure. Later, you will be stopped at Johnston Canyon to enjoy the real fun.
