Enchanting Bali Tour

Embark on an enchanting journey through Bali's captivating landscapes and rich cultural heritage. Immerse yourself in pristine beaches, lush rice terraces, ancient temples, and vibrant local traditions on our unforgettable Bali tour.

Learn more
Day 1 : Arrive at Bali - Tanjung Benoa Beach
After reaching Bali, our local representative will greet you at the airport and take you to your hotel. Since Bali is famous for its fabulous beaches, begin your trip by visiting Tanjung Benoa Beach, where you can stroll at a leisurely pace, immersing yourself in beachy activities. If you love watersports, choose from parasailing, banana boat riding, and jet skiing. Pamper yourself in the afternoon with two hours of a rejuvenating spa treatment. Spend the night at the hotel.
Day 2 : Batubulan Village- Kintamani Volcano Safari
Commence the second day with a visit to Batubulan Village, known for its art exhibits. You can also witness a Barong dance performance in this village. Make sure to experience the Kecak Dance as well while you’re there. Next comes the Kintamani Volcano Safari, where you can enjoy the breathtaking view of the hot black lava of Mount Batur. See the rice terraces, coffee plantations, and ancient temples before returning to your hotel.
Day 3 : Bedugul - Bali Botanical Garden - Ulun Danu Beratan Temple
Bedugul is a mountain lake resort area and the first stop on the third day of your Balinese holiday. Visit the Bali Botanical Garden, the most extensive botanical garden in Indonesia, with the biggest collection of wild orchids on the island. For some spiritual experience, visit the Ulun Danu Beratan Temple, dedicated to the Hindu Gods Brahma, Vishnu, and Shiva.
Day 4 : Taman Mini Indonesia Indah
In the 4th day, you will visit Taman Mini, a significant point of Indonesia’s rich natural and cultural diversity. Situated in East Jakarta, it contains 26 replicas of traditional houses of chieftains related to Indonesian history. Sprawling over 250 acres, you can find several flora and fauna parks, museums, performing theatres, accommodation facilities, and an Imax theatre.
Day 5 : Shopping – Depart from Jakarta
After breakfast, visit a market of your choice, such as the modern and eclectic Pasar Santa or the Tanah Abang Market, the largest textile market in Southeast Asia. Post a round of shopping, you will be transferred to the airport, from where you can board your flight.
